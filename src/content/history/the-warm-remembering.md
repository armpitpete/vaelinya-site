The Warm Remembering is not a dark ritual. It is a way of keeping love, names, stories, and useful memory close.

In Vaelinya, death is not treated as failure or disappearance. It is part of the long pattern of care. A person can be gone from daily touch and still remain present in story, place, song, work, and remembered kindness.

This should stay positive in tone. It may be sad, but it should not become grim.
